Learn to Play Quiddler

OBJECT: To obtain the highest number of points by combining the cards in your hand into words.

ROUNDS: 8. With each round, the cards dealt increases by one.

THE DEAL: After each deal, the remaining cards are placed in the center as a draw pile, and the top card is turned over to start the discard pile.

GOING OUT: A player can only go out during when they can use the number of cards they were dealt in a word. After drawing, a player must be able to arrange all the cards with one card remaining which must be discarded to go out.
